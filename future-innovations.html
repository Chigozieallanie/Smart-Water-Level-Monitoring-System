Roadmap to Smarter Water

Mobile Integration

Develop a comprehensive mobile application for remote monitoring, customized alerts, and historical data analysis. Featuring intuitive dashboards and user-friendly controls.

Explore solar power options to make the system self-sustainable and ideal for off-grid locations or areas with unreliable power supply.
